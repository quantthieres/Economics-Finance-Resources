# Courses

Curated courses, specializations, learning paths and practical training
programs related to economics, finance, data analysis and quantitative methods.

## Finance and Quantitative Modeling

- [Introduction to the Stock Options Market](https://www.udemy.com/course/introducao-ao-mercado-de-opcoes-sobre-acoes/) - Portuguese-language introductory course on stock options, their mechanics and common strategies.

- [Finance & Quantitative Modeling for Analysts](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/finance-quantitative-modeling-analysts) - Coursera specialization combining financial analysis, business modeling, spreadsheets and quantitative methods.

## Data Analytics

- [Introduction to Microsoft Data Analytics](https://learn.microsoft.com/pt-br/training/paths/data-analytics-microsoft/) - Microsoft Learn pathway introducing data analytics, Microsoft Fabric and concepts relevant to data-analyst certification.

## Quantitative Finance and Career Simulations

- [J.P. Morgan Quantitative Research Job Simulation](https://www.theforage.com/simulations/jpmorgan/quantitative-research-11oc) - Free, self-paced simulation of quantitative-research tasks in financial engineering, data analytics and risk modeling.
