# Quantitative Finance

Curated papers and learning resources on quantitative investing, market
microstructure, algorithmic trading, derivatives and financial modeling.

## Quantitative Research and Trading

- [Data-Driven Trade Flow Decomposition for Exchange-Traded Funds and their Constituents](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=6240179) - Data-driven analysis of trading flows between ETFs and their constituent securities.

- [Performance v. Turnover: A Story by 4,000 Alphas](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=2657603) - Examination of the relationship between alpha performance, turnover and trading costs.

- [The Probability of Backtest Overfitting](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=2326253) - Framework for measuring the risk that an investment strategy has been overfitted during backtesting.

## Market Making and Market Microstructure

- [Dynamic Slippage Control and Rejection Feedback in Spot FX Market Making](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.07752) - Dynamic control of slippage and rejected trades in foreign-exchange market making.

- [Market Making with Alpha Signals](https://ora.ox.ac.uk/objects/uuid:c2ba6656-8eab-4b2e-a24a-e9e842d1378f/files/s41687h481) - Market-making strategies that incorporate predictive alpha signals.

- [Market Making by an FX Dealer: Tiers, Pricing Ladders and Hedging Rates for Optimal Risk Control](https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.02269) - Optimal pricing and hedging decisions for a dealer operating across different FX client tiers.

- [Price Dynamics in a Markovian Limit Order Market](https://arxiv.org/abs/1104.4596) - Mathematical model of price formation and order-book dynamics in a limit-order market.

## Derivatives, Hedging and Volatility

- [Deep Hedging: Hedging Derivatives Under Generic Market Frictions Using Reinforcement Learning](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3355706) - Reinforcement-learning approach to derivative hedging under transaction costs and other market frictions.

- [Multi-Asset Spot and Option Market Simulation](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3980817) - Framework for simulating joint spot and option markets across multiple assets.

- [The Joint S&P 500/VIX Smile Calibration Puzzle Solved](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3397382) - Joint modeling and calibration of S&P 500 and VIX option-implied volatility smiles.

- [Volatility Is (Mostly) Path-Dependent](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=4174589) - Evidence and modeling approaches emphasizing the path dependence of market volatility.

## Asset Pricing and Factor Investing

- [Common Risk Factors in the Returns on Stocks and Bonds](https://doi.org/10.1016/0304-405X(93)90023-5) - Fama and French's foundational three-factor model for stock and bond returns.

- [On Persistence in Mutual Fund Performance](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1540-6261.1997.tb03808.x) - Introduces the momentum factor commonly associated with the Carhart four-factor model.

- [Time Series Momentum](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jfineco.2011.11.003) - Evidence of trend persistence across equity-index, currency, commodity and bond futures.

- [Betting Against Beta](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jfineco.2013.10.005) - Studies the performance of leveraged low-beta portfolios under investor funding constraints.

- [Empirical Asset Pricing via Machine Learning](https://academic.oup.com/rfs/article/33/5/2223/5758276) - Comparative application of machine-learning methods to return prediction and empirical asset pricing.

- [Characteristics Are Covariances: A Unified Model of Risk and Return](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jfineco.2019.05.009) - Links firm characteristics, factor exposures and expected returns within a unified asset-pricing framework.

## Statistical Arbitrage

- [Pairs Trading: Performance of a Relative-Value Arbitrage Rule](https://doi.org/10.1093/rfs/hhj020) - Empirical evaluation of a distance-based pairs-trading strategy using historical US equity data.

## Foundational Market Microstructure

- [Continuous Auctions and Insider Trading](https://www.jstor.org/stable/1913210) - Kyle's foundational model of informed trading, liquidity and price impact.

- [Bid, Ask and Transaction Prices in a Specialist Market with Heterogeneously Informed Traders](https://doi.org/10.1016/0304-405X(85)90044-3) - Foundational model connecting asymmetric information with bid–ask spreads and transaction prices.
